Are you Old enough

Prompt the user for their age, then log a response to the console, depending on their age:
  • If their age is negtive or not a number, print an error message.
  • If their age is less than 16, print, "Won't someone Please think of the children? "
  • If their age is at least 16 but less than 18, print, "Sorry, you're not old enough to come in. Drive on home, kid!"
  • If their age is at least 18 but less than 21, print, "Come on in, but no drinking"
  • If their age is 21 or greater, print, "Okey, get smashed."

In Addition, print the following message that ate true:
  • If their age is 21, print "Happy 21st Birthday!"
  • If their age is odd, print "You're an odd one...."
  • If their age is perfect square, print "Holy crap, you're a prefect square!"

Example 1: 13 => "Won't someone Please think of the children! You're an odd one...
Example 1: 49 => "Okey, get smashed. You're an odd one.. Holy crap, you're a prefect square!"
Example 1: "thirty" => "Error: Please enter a valid number."
