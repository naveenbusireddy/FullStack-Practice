Fortune Teller

Write a function called tellFortune that takes 4 arguments:

  • Number of Childern
  • Partner's Name
  • Location
  • Job Title

function should print out the sentance in the following format:

"You will be a $job title in $location, married to $partner with $childern kids"

Ex. "You will be a shopping cart reallocation technician in Cucamonga, CA, and married to Donald Trump with 17 kids"
